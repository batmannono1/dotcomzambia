What is a Mobile POS Machine?

A mobile POS is portable machine that allows you to perform cash register functions like make sales and accept payments without being connected to a direct power source.

Who is a Mobile POS system for?

• Businesses/Organizations that operate in mobile locations
• Businesses/Organizations that want to collect payments in the field
• Businesses/Organizations that to improve the customer experience and embrace the future.
• Businesses/Organizations that want to streamline the sales process

With a Mobile POS system
you can:

> Make Money in More Places

•Make sales in the field or on the go. Break the cord and start taking payments anywhere and everywhere.

•Take multiple payment methods from customers - Your customers can pay through whatever methods you choose, whether cash, by card, check or even using a smart card payment system.

> Manage Your Business While Keeping Track of the Bottomline

• Manage your business more effectively and set your sales team free. Monitor sales and staff performances from multiple devices in one system.

• With streamlined connectivity, you can keep track of your business’s sales regardless of where your sales team is. Even if they do not have an internet connection, you can still make sales and sync once internet connectivity is restored.

• Track Sales and Inventory in real time and know when you need to restock (for retailers)

> Grow and Expand Your Operations Quickly

• With our simple to set up solutions, setting up a new sales person or outlet takes minutes instead of days.

• Expand your business and keep sales teams growing as your business does with mobility and up to 2 day battery life on POS machines.*

* Actual battery life may vary based on application and usage.
